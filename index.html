About Me

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Google, where I work on cutting-edge problems in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Previously, I was a Researcher at Microsoft Research, contributing to the Holoportation project. My academic journey includes a PhD from the University of Alicante, specializing in real-time 3D reconstruction and depth sensing.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between physical and digital worlds through advanced depth estimation, volumetric video, and neural rendering techniques.
